Immediate Steps After Storm Damage

Safety should always be your first priority when assessing storm damage:

  • Stay clear of downed power lines
  • Document damage with photos for insurance purposes
  • Check for structural damage to your home
  • Assess tree damage from a safe distance

Professional Storm Damage Assessment

Our storm damage cleanup and restoration experts recommend professional assessment for:

  • Leaning or partially uprooted trees
  • Split or cracked major limbs
  • Hanging branches over structures
  • Multiple damaged trees

Tree Recovery and Prevention

Not all storm-damaged trees need removal. Our tree cabling and bracing services can often save valuable trees. Learn more about preventive measures in our guide to tree stability in Mooresville.

Long-term Recovery Solutions

After immediate cleanup, consider these steps:

  • Stump removal for fallen trees
  • Soil restoration around damaged areas
  • Replanting with storm-resistant species
  • Regular seasonal maintenance to prevent future damage
